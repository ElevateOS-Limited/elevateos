FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

How does the 7-day free trial work? ▾
You get full access to your chosen plan for 7 days — completely free. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial you can choose to subscribe or simply walk away with the plan you've built.
Is Edutech useful if I don't know what I want to study? ▾
Absolutely — in fact we built an entire "exploration" pathway specifically for undecided students. Check the "I'm not sure yet" option on your intake form and the AI will prioritize activities that help you discover your passion through real experience.
What grade should I start in? ▾
Grade 9 is ideal — the earlier you start building your profile, the more time you have to accumulate meaningful experiences. That said, even Grade 12 students have found Edutech valuable for strengthening their final applications.
Are the opportunities real or made up? ▾
All recommended programs, competitions, and organizations (MIT PRIMES, Google Science Fair, DECA, HOSA, etc.) are real and actively running. We include direct links and instructions on how to get involved.
Can I change my plan later? ▾
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
